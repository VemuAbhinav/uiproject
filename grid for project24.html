SEDAN

A sedan is a passenger car in a three-box configuration with separate compartments for an engine, passengers, and cargo.

The first recorded use of the word "sedan" in reference to an automobile body occurred in 1912.

HATCHBACK

A hatchback is a car with an extra door at the back which opens upward.

A hatchback is called a liftback when the opening area is very sloped and is lifted up to open.

SUV

Sports Utility Vehicle SUV stands for Sports Utility Vehicle, a car similar to a minivan or a station wagon, but with a much tougher look and a design suited for off-road driving.

SUV is often categorized under RV(Recreational Vehicle), but it is sometimes used in exchange.

MUV

MUV stands for Multi Utility Vehicle example Tata Sumo,Mahindra Bolero etc which can be used for both passengers and luggage. SUV stands for Sport Utility Vehicle meant for usage in all road conditions like level road,uneven roads,hilly areas, etc .

CONVERTIBLE

A car whose top can be folded back or removed so that there is no roof.
